How it Started

We began the project at the beginning of 2024 to create an online budgeting application. We kept the design simple, using a simple color scheme and keeping it easy to learn.

What is Easy Budget?

Easy Budget is a budgeting application that lets you keep track of your financial accounts and goals, while providing overviews on their status. It lets you enter your financial accounts and transactions for each account. Then, it can show you a detailed list of your accounts and transactions, or it can give you simple summaries and statistics.

Keeping it Simple

Easy Budget sticks to the basics: letting you record your information and displaying simple overviews and tables. It is easy to learn, use, and read - just log in and enter your transactions.
